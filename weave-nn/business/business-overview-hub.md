## Key Insights

### Target Market

**Primary**: AI-first development teams managing large volumes of AI-generated documentation

**Secondary**: Knowledge workers, researchers, technical writers using AI tools daily

**Positioning**: Local-first alternative to cloud-first knowledge management (Notion, Confluence, Roam)

### Monetization Strategy

**Freemium Model**:
1. **Local-first core** - Always free (Obsidian vault + Weaver service)
2. **Cloud sync** - Paid tier (multi-device, collaboration)
3. **Enterprise features** - Custom pricing (SSO, compliance, SLA)

**Revenue Drivers**:
- Cloud sync subscriptions ($10-20/month per user)
- Enterprise licenses ($500-2000/month per team)
- Professional services (custom integrations, training)
