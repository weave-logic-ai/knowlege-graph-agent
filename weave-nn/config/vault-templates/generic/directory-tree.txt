Customization Instructions
===========================

This generic template provides a minimal starting structure.
Customize by adding:

1. Additional directories for your specific needs
2. More technical documentation in technical/
3. Detailed architecture documents in architecture/
4. Feature specifications in features/
5. Decision records as you make them
6. Custom workflows for your development process

The structure is intentionally minimal to allow maximum flexibility.

Estimated Nodes: 15-35 | Connections: 30-70 | Clusters: 3-5
